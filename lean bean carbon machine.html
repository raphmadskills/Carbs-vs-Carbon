Lean, Bean Carbon Machine

The Bread: Seeded Bap The filling: Lentils have a very low carbon footprint up to 43 time less than beef — and require little water to grow. They also clean and fortify soil to make it easier to grow other crops. Legume crops like beans also help build soil. The crunch: Foraged leaves / microgreens or homegrown salad – if in doubt just go for organic salad or lettuce!
